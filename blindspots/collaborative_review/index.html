Perspectives

Researcher: I am the expert in this area. Anything I produce should not be reviewed or questioned.

Practitioner: I need to review research outputs because I will apply them. I need to understand where the statements came from so I can apply them appropriately.

Convergence: Researchers and practitioners have complimentary skill sets. For research to be applied and influence practice, the two need to work as a team. Researchers need to be open to accepting feedback from pracitioners because it can improve the impact of the research.
